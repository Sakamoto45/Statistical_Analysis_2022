Prestige, income, education and suicide rates for 36 occupations (1950)

Labovitz, S. (1970) The assignment of numbers to rank order categories.
American Sociological Review, 35, p.515-524, Table 1.

The sourse paper was concerned with the effect of treating ordinal
scales as intervals.

Variables
OCCUPATION - occupation (no)
PRESTIGE   - the NORC occupational prestige rating
SUICIDE    - the male suicide rate (amongst males aged 20-64)
INCOME     - the median income ($)
SCHOOL     - the median number of school years completed
